Battery Storage

Store the solar energy you generate during the day and use it at night. Battery storage maximises your self-consumption, reduces reliance on the grid, and keeps your home powered during outages.

As certified installers for both Tesla Powerwall and Duracell Energy, we offer industry-leading battery solutions designed to integrate seamlessly with your solar system.

  • Tesla Powerwall -- 13.5 kWh capacity, backup gateway included
  • Duracell Energy -- modular design, scalable from 5 kWh
  • Smart time-of-use tariff integration (Octopus, etc.)
  • Retrofit to existing solar or install alongside new panels
  • Real-time monitoring via smartphone app
