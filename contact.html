Common Questions

How much does solar cost?

Every home is different, but typical systems range from £5,000–£12,000. We provide a free, no-obligation quote tailored to your home.

Do you cover my area?

We cover all of Scotland, from the Borders to the Highlands. If you're not sure, just ask!

How long does installation take?

Most residential installations are completed in 2–4 days with minimal disruption to your daily life.

Can you maintain panels you didn't install?

Absolutely. Our £99 health-check and Vixen Care Plan are available regardless of who installed your system.
