Our Story

Built on trust,
driven by quality

Founded in 2019, GreenFox Energy grew from a shared frustration with the solar industry. Too many companies were offering one-size-fits-all solutions, confusing customers with technical jargon, and disappearing after installation.

We set out to do things differently. Every home gets a proper survey. Every customer gets plain-English advice. Every system is designed specifically for that household's needs. And every installation is carried out by our own in-house team — never subcontracted.
