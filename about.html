About Us

People-centric solar, from Scotland

GreenFox Energy was founded with a simple mission: to make solar energy accessible, understandable, and genuinely helpful for Scottish homeowners. No jargon, no hard sell -- just honest advice and quality installations.

Our Story

Built on trust, driven by quality

Founded in 2019, GreenFox Energy grew from a shared frustration with the solar industry. Too many companies were offering one-size-fits-all solutions, confusing customers with technical jargon, and disappearing after installation.

We set out to do things differently. Every home gets a proper survey. Every customer gets plain-English advice. Every system is designed specifically for that household's needs. And every installation is carried out by our own in-house team -- never subcontracted.

Today, we're one of Scotland's most trusted solar companies, with a 4.7-star Trustpilot rating and accreditations from MCS, Which?, RECC, and Trustmark. But what we're most proud of is the relationships we've built with our customers -- many of whom have referred us to their friends and family.

Building relationships, not just systems

From day one to year twenty-five, we're here for you. That's the GreenFox promise.
