Why It Matters

Don't let your panels underperform

Dirty, damaged, or unmaintained solar panels can lose 15-20% of their efficiency. Bird droppings, pollen, moss, and general weathering all take their toll -- and in Scotland's climate, regular care makes a real difference.

Most solar companies disappear after installation. At GreenFox, we believe the relationship is just beginning. The Vixen Care Plan is how we ensure your system delivers maximum value for its entire 25+ year lifespan.
