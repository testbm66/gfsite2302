User Journeys

Four representative buyer types and how the site serves each one today.

A
New solar buyer — no panels yet
Homepage / Services → Get Free Quote → Quote funnel (location → property → system size → battery → timeline → postcode → contact → package → order summary)

What works

  • Consistent "Get Free Quote" CTA across every page
  • Funnel skips redundant step 1; starts at location
  • Progress bar, Trustpilot card, and fox helper build trust
  • Package step shows batteries, add-ons, bird protection included

What needs fixing

  • Funnel end still shows deposit/Stripe checkout (Boss #8)
  • HubSpot, Mapbox, and Stripe placeholders prevent live use
Homepage — hero & primary CTA
Quote funnel — location step
B
Existing solar — battery or EV charger
Services → Get Battery Quote or Enquire About EV Charging → quote.html (same new-solar funnel)

What works

  • Services page clearly explains battery & EV offerings
  • Contact form has "Battery storage enquiry" in subject dropdown

What needs fixing

  • Critical Both CTAs dump users into the new-solar funnel — intent is lost
  • No dedicated battery or EV pages exist yet (Boss #5, #15, #16)
Services page — Battery & EV CTAs route to wrong funnel
C
Existing solar — care plan or health check
Nav Health-Check → solar-healthcheck.html  |  Footer / Services → Vixen Care Plan → vixen-care-plan.html

What works

  • Clear separation: £99 health-check vs ongoing Vixen Care Plan
  • Card order correct (Vixen left, One-Off right — Boss #12)
  • Contact form includes health-check and care plan subjects

What needs fixing

  • "Book Health-Check" and "Enquire Now" need to connect to a real booking/contact flow

Recommended Actions

Prioritised list of what to tackle before go-live.

High priority — blockers
  • Remove deposit/Stripe from funnel end. Replace with "Send me my quote" (HubSpot email) and "Speak to someone" (contact/callback).
  • Create dedicated battery and EV charger pages with their own info + contact paths. Point existing CTAs to them.
  • Replace every YOUR_* / YOURDOMAIN.COM placeholder with live values (HubSpot portal, form GUID, Mapbox token, domain).
Medium priority
  • Externalise pricing (Boss #20) so updates don’t need code changes.
  • Wire "Book Health-Check" and "Enquire Now" to a real contact/booking flow.
  • Confirm Contact form submits to HubSpot once portal ID is set.
Nice to have
  • Unhide Care & Maintenance card on home/services when offer is ready.
  • Add "Battery Storage" link to area page footers for consistency.
