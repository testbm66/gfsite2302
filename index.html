How It Works

Our easy 5-step process to going solar

If the nuts and bolts of solar power are all Greek to you, don't worry, GreenFox is dedicated to making things agreeably clear.

1

Understanding your energy needs

We visit your home for a free survey to understand your energy usage and assess the best solar solution for your property.

2

Informative, jargon-free advice

We explain everything in plain English, design a system that fits your needs, and give you an honest quote with no hidden costs.

3

Supply & install your solution

Our MCS-certified team installs your fully compliant and tested system in 2-4 days, with minimal disruption to your daily life.

4

Registration & energy exporting

We handle all the paperwork, register your system, and set you up to export excess energy back to the grid and earn money.

5

Ongoing monitoring & support

Unlike others, installation is just the start. We monitor, optimise, and maintain your system for years to come.
