How It Works

Your 5-step journey to solar

If the nuts and bolts of solar power are all Greek to you, don't worry. GreenFox is dedicated to making things agreeably clear. Here's exactly what happens from first contact to generating free energy.

1

Understanding your energy needs

It all starts with a conversation. We'll visit your home for a free, no-obligation survey to understand how you use energy, assess your roof, and work out the best solar solution for your property.

  • Full roof assessment (orientation, shading, structural suitability)
  • Review of your energy bills and usage patterns
  • Discussion of your goals (bill reduction, independence, EV charging)
  • No drones or Google Earth guesswork -- we visit in person
2

Informative, jargon-free advice

We design a system specifically for your home and explain everything in plain English. You'll receive a detailed, honest quote with no hidden costs and no pressure to decide on the spot.

  • Custom system design with predicted generation figures
  • Clear breakdown of costs, savings, and payback period
  • Battery storage and EV charger options if relevant
  • Answers to all your questions -- take as long as you need
3

Supply & install your solution

Once you're happy to proceed, our MCS-certified in-house team handles everything. Typical installations take 2-4 days, with minimal disruption to your daily life.

  • Premium panels, inverters, and components
  • Installed by our own trained engineers -- never subcontracted
  • Full scaffold, safety compliance, and site cleanup
  • System tested and commissioned before we leave
4

Registration & energy exporting

We handle all the paperwork so you don't have to. Your system is registered with the MCS, your DNO is notified, and we set you up to export surplus energy back to the grid.

  • MCS registration and compliance documentation
  • DNO (Distribution Network Operator) notification
  • Smart Export Guarantee (SEG) setup -- earn money from surplus energy
  • Insurance Backed Guarantee (IBG) certificate provided
5

Ongoing monitoring & support

Unlike many installers, installation is just the beginning of our relationship. We monitor your system's performance, optimise it over time, and provide ongoing care through our Vixen Care Plan.

  • Remote system monitoring and performance alerts
  • Annual health-check option
  • Panel cleaning and maintenance services
  • Priority support for Vixen Care Plan members
  • We're with you for the life of your system
